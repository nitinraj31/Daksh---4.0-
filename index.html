FAQ

Quick answers to common registration and event questions.

How do I register for DAKSH - 4.0?➕

Use the Registration section, fill your details, and select an event category. Submit to receive a success popup and QR option.

Who is eligible to participate?➕

Students from recognized institutions are welcome. Eligibility rules may vary by event category—check guidelines at submission and notice updates.

What are the rules & regulations?➕

Participants must adhere to event-specific rules, timelines, and conduct. Failure to follow guidelines may lead to disqualification.

Do we get certificates & prizes?➕

Eligible participants receive certificates. Top performers receive awards/prizes as per judging and event criteria.
